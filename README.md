**Bellabeat Consumer Behavior Analysis**

An end-to-end case study exploring smart-device activity data to identify consumer behavior patterns and marketing opportunities for Bellabeat, a wellness technology company.

**Business Task**

Analyze Fitbit activity data to understand how consumers use smart devices and develop data-driven recommendations that could support Bellabeat’s marketing strategy.

**Tools Used**

SQL / BigQuery

Tableau Public

Kaggle

Google Sheets

**Process**

1. Inspected and cleaned two daily activity datasets in BigQuery.
2. Documented data-quality checks and cleaning decisions.
3. Combined the cleaned datasets using UNION ALL.
4. Conducted exploratory analysis of activity levels, daily step patterns, sedentary time, and the relationship between steps and calories burned.
5. Created an interactive Tableau dashboard and story to communicate findings.
6. Developed marketing recommendations based on observed consumer behavior.

**Key Findings**

-Most users fell into low-to-moderate activity categories.

-Users spent substantially more time sedentary or lightly active than highly active.

-Daily activity varied throughout the week, without a consistent weekend pattern.

-Higher daily step counts generally corresponded with higher calorie expenditure.

**Recommendations**

-Position Bellabeat as a wellness-first lifestyle brand rather than exclusively a performance-fitness product.

-Focus engagement campaigns on lightly active users who are building sustainable health habits.

-Emphasize personalized goals, app customization, and long-term wellness tracking.

-Highlight everyday movement and holistic health features, including sleep and cycle tracking.
